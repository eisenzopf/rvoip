UCTP WebSocket browser smoke (older-browser fallback)

Opens a plain WebSocket against the orchestrator_bridge at ws://127.0.0.1:7777, sends one auth.hello envelope, prints the auth.challenge reply.

Unlike the WebTransport variant, plain WS doesn't need cert pinning — works in any browser without flags. This is the fallback path for older browsers and constrained networks per CONVERSATION_PROTOCOL.md §4.3.
